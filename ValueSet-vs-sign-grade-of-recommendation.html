SystemCodeDisplay (en)DefinitionJSONXML
http://fhir.awmf.org/awmf.ig/CodeSystem/cs-sign-grade-of-recommendation  AGrade AAt least one meta-analysis, systematic review, or RCT rated as 1++, and directly applicable to the target population; or a body of evidence consisting principally of studies rated as 1+, directly applicable to the target population, and demonstrating overall consistency of results.
http://fhir.awmf.org/awmf.ig/CodeSystem/cs-sign-grade-of-recommendation  BGrade BA body of evidence including studies rated as 2++, directly applicable to the target population, and demonstrating overall consistency of results; or extrapolated evidence from studies rated as 1++ or 1+.
http://fhir.awmf.org/awmf.ig/CodeSystem/cs-sign-grade-of-recommendation  CGrade CA body of evidence including studies rated as 2+, directly applicable to the target population and demonstrating overall consistency of results; or extrapolated evidence from studies rated as 2++.
http://fhir.awmf.org/awmf.ig/CodeSystem/cs-sign-grade-of-recommendation  DGrade DEvidence level 3 or 4; or extrapolated evidence from studies rated as 2+; or consensus-based recommendation.
